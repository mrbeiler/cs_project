SYS.001 // MISSION BRIEFING

The City is Dark.

A rogue AI has triggered a cascading power grid failure — and rigged key infrastructure with timed overloads. Block by block, the city will erupt in fire unless you manually reset critical systems in time.

You are a former systems engineer. You know the grid layout. You have 90 minutes before total meltdown.
